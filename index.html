Biography

Hi, I'm Arthur MAILLART, a PhD candidate from the SAF laboratory affiliated to the University of Lyon 1 . After graduating as an actuary, I decided to get into the adventure of a CIFRE PhD thesis on a topic that I am interested deeply in, the interpretability of Machine Learning models. Currently, in my third year of PhD thesis, I am working under the supervision of Christian Y. Robert on methods to improve the understanding of Machine Learning model outputs.
